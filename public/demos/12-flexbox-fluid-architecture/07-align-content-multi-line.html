Align-Content & Justify-Content
Axes: main row → x cross column → y

Tip: justify-content always works on items along the main axis. align-content works on the stack of lines on the cross axis — and only when you have wrapping, multiple lines, and extra space.

LAB CONTROLS
flex-wrap: wrap
Turn wrapping off to see single-line behavior.

Watch for the classic gotcha: justify-content still works on items in each line. align-content only kicks in when you have wrapping, multiple lines, and cross-axis space to distribute.
