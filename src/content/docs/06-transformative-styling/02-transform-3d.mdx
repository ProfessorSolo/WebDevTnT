# **Bending Space in the Third Dimension**

Time to leave the flatlands, folks.

This is where our elements stop behaving like stickers and start acting like **objects in actual space**.

No animation yet ‚Äî just posing, tilting, flipping, and revealing surfaces our UI never had before.

---

## üéõ The 3D Tools

In 3D land, you‚Äôll mostly use:

- `translateZ()` ‚Äî move toward / away from the viewer
- `rotateX()` ‚Äî tilt forward / back
- `rotateY()` ‚Äî turn left / right
- `rotateZ()` ‚Äî spin like 2D rotation
- `perspective` ‚Äî the camera that makes depth visible

---

## ü™Ç Perspective: the depth-maker

Here‚Äôs the rule:  
**Perspective goes on the parent**, not the thing rotating.

It's hard to maintain perspective from the inside.

```css
.stage {
  perspective: 800px;
}
```

Without perspective, rotateX/rotateY look flat and kinda sad.

Our demo stage already has perspective baked in.
